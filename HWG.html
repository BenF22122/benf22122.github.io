What we do

Havant Wargaming Club is a not-for-profit club serving the local area. We focus on friendly gaming nights where you can roll dice and push models.

Affordable by design: First game is free, then as little as £2.50

"Whether you're a seasoned veteran or a new recruit, you're welcome here."
